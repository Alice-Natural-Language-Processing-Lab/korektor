CMake
=====

CMake at least 2.8 is required.

CMake options:
- UNIX / WIN32 (set automatically)
- CMAKE_BUILD_TYPE={Release,Debug}

- UNIX & WITH_REST_SERVER: Also build REST server.
- UNIX & STATIC_LINKING: Link libstdc++ and libgcc statically.
- UNIX & WITH_REST_SERVER & STATIC_LINKING: Link libmicrohttpd statically.

- USE_CLANG: use clang
  - USE_CLANG & USE_CXX: use libc++
- USE_ICC: use icc
- USE_GPROF: compile with -pg
